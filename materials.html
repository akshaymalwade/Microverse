About these documents

Some of the documents on this page (the daily readings, the primer, and the deep reference) were drafted with the help of an AI system. They have been reviewed and edited, but specific factual claims — dates, citations, numerical estimates — should be checked against primary sources before being cited in any written work. The baseline test and schedule are instructor-authored.
