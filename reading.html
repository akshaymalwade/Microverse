  • Week 1 · Culturing
    Bacterial culture through selective and non-selective conditions: the evolution of culture media in clinical microbiology
    Bonnet M, Lagier JC, Raoult D, Khelaifia S · New Microbes and New Infections, 34, 100622 (2020)

    A review of how culture media have evolved from Pasteur's first liquid medium in 1860 to modern selective and enrichment media. Good background for the first week of the course — why we use agar, why specific nutrients, why some microbes grow and others do not.
