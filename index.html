Project Microverse

A microbiology lab module · 18 May – 12 June 2026

Over four weeks, you will take a swab from your finger — or a sample from somewhere else — and follow it through five techniques until a species name appears on a screen. Culturing, colony PCR, gel electrophoresis, Sanger sequencing, BLAST.
