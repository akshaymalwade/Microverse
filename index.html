Project Microverse

An undergraduate microbiology lab module · 18 May – 12 June 2026

Over four weeks, you will take a swab from your finger — or a sample from somewhere else — and follow it through five techniques until a species name appears on a screen. Culturing, colony PCR, gel electrophoresis, Sanger sequencing, BLAST. This page is the day-to-day home for the course.

How you'll be evaluated

Evaluation rests on three components: (1) attendance and engagement across the four weeks; (2) the project — your sample, your pipeline, your group's presentation; (3) the end-of-course test, taken on the final day. The baseline test on Day 1 is not graded; it exists to measure how your understanding changes over the course.
